Customer Behavior

The Customer Behavior Dashboard offers a dynamic and in-depth exploration of how customers engage with the e-commerce platform. It provides critical insights into conversion rates, traffic patterns, cart abandonment, daily average new visitors, and customer demographics, enabling data-driven strategies to enhance user experiences and drive sales growth.

Sales/Revenue

The Sales/Revenue Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of sales and revenue performance, featuring data on potential revenue, revenue per week, total revenue, average order value, and purchase distribution by price points. It provides insights into revenue patterns by weekday and month, alongside channel-specific traffic data, and offers customized data exploration through date range, state, and product selection for data-driven strategies.

  • Full Story

Promotion Effects

The Promotion Effects Dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of promotions' impact on e-commerce, highlighting the correlation between discounts and the total number of products to which each discount is applied. It further includes data on revenue generated by various discounts, purchase patterns under each discount, and traffic generated by discounts across brands and categories, empowering informed decisions in crafting targeted promotional strategies.

  • Full Story

Price Variations

The Price Variations Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of pricing dynamics, including fluctuations by brand, category, hour, and channel, empowering data-driven pricing strategies to maximize revenue. It also supports specific product analysis and provides insights into price trends by weekday, state, and month.

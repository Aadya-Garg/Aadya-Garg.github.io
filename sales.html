Sales/Revenue Dashboard

Introduction:
The Sales/Revenue Dashboard serves as a critical tool for understanding the revenue streams and sales performance. Analyzing these aspects is crucial for developing strategies to enhance revenue, optimize pricing, and allocate resources effectively.

Revenue Metrics:

  1. Weekly Revenue : Monitoring weekly revenue performance is vital for tracking trends and identifying peak sales periods. Week 40 - 46 saw revenues around the average. Revenues peaked in Week 47 (November 20 to November 26) with 1.27M USD, followed by a drastic drop in the following weeks. The promotions, product launches, and prices should be studied for Week 47 to understand what brings in the most traffic and revenue.
  2. Total Revenue : The total revenue generated during the analyzed period was 5.65M USD, reflecting the overall financial health of the platform.
  3. Potential Revenue : The potential revenue identified was 56.72M USD, indicating opportunities for expansion and optimization. It is concerningly higher than the actual revenue by 51.07M USD. Estimating the untapped revenue potential allows us to strategize revenue growth.
  4. Daily Average Revenue: The average revenue generated each day is 94.18K USD.

Average Order Value:

    In the analyzed period, the average order value was 406.12 USD, suggesting potential areas for optimizing product bundles and increasing customer spending.

Purchases per Price:

    Analyzing the distribution of purchases across different price points reveals customer preferences and can guide pricing strategies.

    For example, maximum purchases occurred in the price range of 0 USD to almost a 1000 USD, indicating strong demand within this price range.

Revenue by Weekday, Month :

  1. Notably, Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays and November showed significant revenue peaks, providing insights for targeted promotional activities. The weekdays align well with insight on Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays being the busiest weeks (Customer Behaviour).

Channel Effectiveness :

  1. Analyzing revenue generation across various channels, alongside the traffic in each channel, provides insights into channel effectiveness. The browser channel generated the highest revenue of about 2.9M, aligning with efforts to maximize profits through this channel. Traffic on each channel is nearly the same (as seen by bubble sizes).

Revenue by Brand, Category, and Subcategory:

  1. Apple accounted for maximum revenue, while category Electronics contributed more than 50% to the overall revenue.

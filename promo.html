Promotion Effects Dashboard

Introduction:
The Promotion Effects Dashboard is a crucial instrument for evaluating the impact of discounts and promotions on the e-commerce business. It enables us to understand how promotions influence customer behavior, traffic, and, most importantly, revenue.

Note: These are slicers for customized analysis based on requirement:

Potential Revenue by Discount, along with number of products the discount is applied on:


  1. Although, 5% is on maximum number of products (bubble size), the potential revenue (based on the analyzed period) is only 1M USD. Contrastingly, 15% can generate a very high revenue of 3M USD but it is applied less than 5%, 10%, and 25%. This suggests, that the platform is more focused on Views traffic than Purchase traffic and points out the specific area of improvement to generate higher revenue.

Purchases per Discount :

  1. Our analysis revealed that a 5-25% discounts encouraged 2325 purchases, indicating a strong correlation between discounts and increased purchase behavior.

Discount effectiveness for each Brand:


  1. Discounts showed a significant increase in revenue mainly for top brands like Samsung, Apple, Xiaomi, Huawei, Oppo. 10% discount on Samsung products contributed to about 4-5K USD and 15% on Apple products also led to a 4-5K USD additional revenue. These further solidify the proposition that 5% discounts are not the most effective discounts, and 10% and 15% discounts should instead be applied to more products.

Promotion effects on Top Category :


  1. Understanding the revenue generated for the most critical category when applying a discount helps in focusing promotional efforts. Highest revenue of over 3M USD was generated by 15% discounts, followed by 25% and 10%. 5% contributed about 1M USD.

Price Variations Dashboard

Introduction:
TThe Price Variations Dashboard is designed to uncover critical insights into the pricing dynamics of the e-commerce platform. By examining price fluctuations across various dimensions, this dashboard equips us with valuable data for making informed pricing decisions and maintaining competitiveness in the market.

Note: These are slicers for customized analysis based on requirement:
1) Product: Can select Category, Subcategory, Brand
2) Event Date
3) Data Range (Event Date)
4) Weekday
5) State
6) Event Type: View,Cart, Purchase.
7) Month
Do note that cross filters provide a more detailed analysis. e.g selecting a specific Channel or Hour.

Price Analysis:

  1. Price variations by Brand and Category: The variations indicate the need for specific pricing strategies for different brands and categories. It helps in setting competitive prices and attracting target audiences effectively. We observe that highest price is seen in MSI and Apple brands. Average price varies from 600 USD to less than 50 USD across categories, with Computers and Electronics being the highest and Stationery the lowest.
  2. Price variations by Hour and Channel : Prices are below average until about 7:00 pm which aligns with high traffic hours (5:00 am to 5:00 pm). It is notable that both the Channels have nearly the same average price (about 390 USD) of products, which seems to be doing well since both channels attract nearly the same amount of traffic. However, on studying the data for specific months, it is seen that there was a notable difference of 7 USD between the average price of both the channels in October, which then reduced in November.
